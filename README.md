## FAQ

**1) Is this vpn software for torrenting safe to leave running all day?**  
Yes—stable connection behavior is the point. Enable **Kill Switch** and keep it on.

**2) Will it slow down my torrent speeds?**  
Any VPN adds overhead. The goal here is **high speed** plus nearby **Global Servers** to keep the hit small.

**3) What stops my real IP from leaking if the VPN drops?**  
The **Kill Switch** blocks traffic when the tunnel fails so the client can’t “fall back” to your ISP route.

**4) What should I enable first after install?**  
Turn on **Kill Switch**, connect to a close server, then verify your public IP before starting a session.
